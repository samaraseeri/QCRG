Welcome to the KAUST Quantum Computing Reading Group

About Us

This webpage serves as a central hub for the KAUST Quantum Computing Reading Group (QCRG). A warm welcome to the Quantum Computation Reading Group at KAUST, led by Professor David Keyes. Explore the fascinating world of quantum computing and its implications for the future of science and technology.

Short-Term Goals

  • Identify thrusts in the KAUST research mission that may benefit currently or in the future from quantum computation
  • Place KAUST on the “on-ramp” for a significant thrust of the Economies of the Future RDIA pillar
  • Propose a “wish list” of speakers for visits to KAUST
  • Prepare the KAUST community to make early use of quantum hardware acquired by NEOM and Aramco
  • Burst the hype bubble of quantum computing and come to a realistic appraisal of its evolving implications

Long-Term Prospects

  • Spawn additional specialty groups
  • Arrive at a syllabus for a three-credit KAUST graduate course in quantum computation
