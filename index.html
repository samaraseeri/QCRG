Welcome to the KAUST Quantum Computing Reading Group

This webpage serves as a central hub for the KAUST Quantum Computing Reading Group (QCRG).

About the Group

The QCRG is a platform for KAUST faculty, students, and researchers interested in exploring the exciting field of quantum computing. We meet regularly to discuss recent research papers, share knowledge, and foster collaboration in this rapidly evolving area.

Our Activities

  • Regular reading group meetings to discuss research papers on quantum computing.
  • Inviting guest speakers to present on cutting-edge topics in quantum computing.
  • Organizing workshops and seminars to promote knowledge sharing and collaboration.
