Quantinuum is pleased to share educational resources for quantum computing

This initiative aims to provide foundational knowledge and practical insights into quantum computing through key documentation and publications.

Available Resources

The following links provide essential documentation and publications:

  • TKET Documentation: Comprehensive guide on using tket, a quantum software development kit.
  • INQUANTO Documentation: Detailed information on INQUANTO, a tool for quantum algorithm design.
  • List of Publications: A collection of research papers and articles related to quantum computing.

Target Audience

These resources are suitable for:

  • Students and researchers interested in quantum computing.
  • Developers looking to implement quantum algorithms using tket and INQUANTO.
  • Anyone seeking to understand the current landscape of quantum computing research.

Getting Started

To explore the resources:

  1. Visit the website for more information.
  2. Access the documentation and publications through the provided links.
