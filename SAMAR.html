Dr. Samar Aseeri

Bio

Dr. Samar Aseeri is a **Computational Scientist** and is virtually a founder of **KAUST** and the **KAUST Supercomputing Lab**. She was at **IBM TJ Watson Lab** in early 2009, before joining KAUST.

She is a **PhD alumna** of **Umm Al-Qura University** in **Applied Mathematics** and is currently completing her **MBA in Artificial Intelligence** at the **University of Cumbria**.

Dr. Aseeri is an expert in HPC math libraries and profiling tools. She is also the co-convenor, alongside **Prof. David Keyes**, of the **Quantum Computing Reading Group** at KAUST, contributing significantly to the field of High Preformance Computing and quantum computing.
