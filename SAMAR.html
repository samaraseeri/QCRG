Dr. Samar Aseeri

Bio

Dr. Samar Aseeri is a Computational Scientist and is virtually a founder of KAUST and the KAUST Supercomputing Lab. She was at IBM TJ Watson Lab in early 2009, before joining KAUST.

She is a PhD alumna of Umm Al-Qura University in Applied Mathematics and is currently completing her MBA in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Cumbria.

Dr. Aseeri is an expert in HPC math libraries and profiling tools, as well as PDEs and spectral methods. She is also the co-convenor, alongside Prof. David Keyes, of the Quantum Computing Reading Group at KAUST, contributing significantly to the field of High Performance Computing and quantum computing.

Finally, Samar is an active member of the HPC community. She has published over 15 papers in top academic journals and conferences and frequently speaks at HPC events. A member of IEEE and ACM, she has attended over 50 HPC conferences to present her work and stay updated on technologies.
