Quantum Computing Platforms

  • Amazon Braket: Access quantum hardware for 12 months free (credit card required).
  • Google Colab: Provides GPUs for quantum computing tasks.
  • IBM Quantum: Offers a free limited usage plan with 4 qubits.
  • Microsoft Azure Quantum: Access to IonQ, Quantinuum, and quantum simulators for accepted projects.
  • D-Wave Leap: Access to D-Wave's quantum computers (availability may vary by region).
  • For Xanadu and Quantinuum, please contact them directly. Quantinuum is also accessible through Azure.
